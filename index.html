What Is It?

Here at Ferris Tech, we are bringing you the Pire Speaker. A normal speaker can be a disturbance in public. This speaker will allow people to share their music in a more intimate setting. Unlike normal sound systems, this speaker only plays sound out to a certain radius, which can be adjusted. If you have ever wanted to enjoy your music with a group of people without disturbing others, this is the product for you.

INSTRUCTIONS

•Charge the speaker using the Micro USB plug on the back.

•Press the range dial to turn on/off the Pire Speaker

•Press and hold for 3 seconds the dial to turn on BlueTooth Mode

•To adjust the volume, glide your finger along the silver ring at the top. Clockwise will increase volume, counterclockwise decreases the volume.

•Use the dial to increase the range of the Pire Speaker. The radius of the sound is shown on the screen above.

•To switch the units shown from feet to meters, flip the switch on the bottom.
